Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

Welcome to CampusCart. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure shopping experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit campuscart.xyz.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Automatically Collected Data: When you visit our site, we automatically collect certain information through Google Analytics, including:
    • Pages you visit and time spent on the site
    • Your device type, browser, and operating system
    • Your approximate location (country/city level)
    • Referral source (how you found us)
  • Order Data: When you place an order through our WhatsApp integration, we collect:
    • Items you order
    • Total amount and delivery fee
    • Your selected campus
    • Your phone number (provided via WhatsApp)
    • Your delivery address (provided via WhatsApp)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Understand which products are popular
  • Analyze website traffic and performance
  • Communicate with you about your order

3. How We Store Your Data

  • Order Data: Order details are stored in our Google Sheets database. We retain this information for business records and order history.
  • Analytics Data: Google Analytics data is stored on Google's servers. We do not store personal identifiers like names or email addresses.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For tracking and analyzing website traffic. Their privacy policy is available at Google Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp: For processing orders. Your phone number and messages are handled through WhatsApp's platform.
  • Google Sheets: For storing order data. Data is stored on Google's secure servers.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique users and track sessions. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Opt-out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We use HTTPS encryption to secure data transmission.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
